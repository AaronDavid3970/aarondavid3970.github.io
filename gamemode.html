Guardian Tales Game Modes

Adventure

Automated PvP mode focused on team strategy. Players navigate through story-driven stages, defeat enemies, solve environmental puzzles, and uncover hidden secrets to progress.

Rewards: Gems, higher rank bonuses.

Arena

Real-time 1v1 PvP, rotating heroes upon defeat. If the first hero is defeated, the next one steps in, continuing until all heroes on one side are defeated.

Rewards: Gems, gold, and seasonal rank rewards.

Guild Raid

Team up with guild members to defeat bosses. Each member's contribution helps in bringing down the boss, and rewards are distributed based on participation and performance.

Rewards: Guild coins, gear, and ranking rewards.

Rift & Mirror Rift

Farm evolution stones and elemental materials. Mirror Rift is divided into elemental categories like Basic, Dark, Light, Water, Earth, and Fire corresponding to the elements of heroes and weapons.

Rewards: Upgrade materials for heroes and weapons.

The Tale of the Tetis Hero

PvE roguelike auto-battler with energy strategy. Heroes fight automatically, but players can manually activate weapon and chain skills if desired

Rewards: Loot, stat boosts, and Guardian Tactic buffs.
