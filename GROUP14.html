What is Guardian Tales?

Guardian Tales is an action-adventure RPG made by Kong Studios and published by Kakao Games. Released in 2020, the game mixes puzzle-solving, exciting battles, and retro pixel-art graphics, giving players a fun and nostalgic experience.

Guardian Tales

Set in the fantasy world of Kanterbury, players become a young Guardian Knight who must protect the kingdom from evil forces. Along the way, they explore dungeons, solve puzzles, fight strong enemies, and collect different heroes, each with their own skills and stories. The game has many gameplay modes, such as a story adventure, co-op missions, PvP battles, and guild events. Players can also build and design their own floating castle, making the game even more enjoyable. With its mix of fun storytelling, humor, and action, Guardian Tales is a great choice for players who love adventure RPGs.
